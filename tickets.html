General Admission ($10)

  • The general admission ticket grants entry to the museum for the entirety of its regular operating hours on the day of the visit. However, it does not provide access to special exhibitions that require a separate ticket.

  • You can buy tickets either in person (subject to exhibition ticket availability) or in advance on (INSERT WEBSITE).org or by calling 1-800-394-7175. Please note that all phone orders are subject to a $6 processing fee per order.

  • Entry time slots are 30 minutes; visitors must enter the exhibition within that 30-minute window.

  • No re-entry into a special exhibition.

  • Visitors are required to enter the exhibition within a 30-minute time slot assigned to them.

Deluxe Admission ($20)

  • Skip the line and enter the museum immediately.

  • Get a more in-depth and engaging experience through a guided tour who will answer any questions you may have about the meaning or history of the art work.

  • Gift shop discounts

  • Entry and ability to switch into one of our three running events

    • 1 hour art lesson under an instructor.
    • 1 hour lecture from a current indegenous artist
    • 1 hour food entry to food banquet featuring indigenous foods

Discounted Admission Programs

  • Students, faculty, and staff from participating colleges and universities can obtain free general admission tickets and special exhibition tickets by presenting a valid student or faculty ID in person. This offer cannot be redeemed in advance.

  • K-12 teachers who are currently working in a school, after-school program, or community setting in Massachusetts, Connecticut, Maine, New Hampshire, Rhode Island, and Vermont can receive a discounted general admission ticket for $15 by showing proof of their active teaching status. Timed tickets for select special exhibitions are available for an additional $7. To receive this discount, teachers can present their school ID or a letter from their principal or director at any ticket desk.

  • Active duty military and veterans of the United States are eligible for free general admission tickets for themselves and up to five guests, as well as $7 special exhibition tickets, when presenting their military or veteran ID in person. This offer cannot be redeemed in advance.
